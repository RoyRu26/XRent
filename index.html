About us Carsharing is a trending topic… Sharing instead of owning is saving resources and connecting people. XRent® is connecting the concept of a normal sports car rental with the advantages of the private car sharing. Especially the luxury car branch in Germany is totally disrupted by these times: Once upon a time it was extraordinarily important to just own a luxury car, even if this one was only driven once a year. Nowadays people at first want to get the experience of an exotic or even a classic car while being totally independent and flexible! There’s still a bit of demand for luxury cars for rent, but nobody wants to bear the financial and time load that an owned car requires in total…
